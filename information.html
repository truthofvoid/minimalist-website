Meaning of the Labyrinth

The Labyrinth represents a journey or path to our own center and back again out into the world. Labyrinths have long been used as meditation and prayer tools. When we walk one it serves as a metaphor for life's journey.

Healing Power of the Labyrinth

Labyrinths have been proven to have the ability to quiet the mind. In good practice, they help connect the body with the mind and the mind with an inner spirit. Finding that inner spirit helps to boost a healthy life in recovery.

Additional Resources

    Malad Website

    Malad City, Idaho is a city in South Eastern Idaho. The Malad Website hopes to provide someone exploring Malad with the resources to do whatever they must do to comfortably navigate Malad City.

    Local Resources

    Idaho Behavioral Health Alliance is a state-wide implement of mental health resources made to support the implementation of behavioral health services in primary care.

    Idaho Suicide and Crisis Line

    The Idaho Suicide and Crisis Line prevents crises by using de-escalation and hopes to prevent any crisis from furthering itself into a danger to others or yourself.

    SEI Behavioral Crisis Center

    The South Eastern Idaho Behavioral Crisis Center is a center for those in need and hopes to create a safe space for all to come to when in need of anything, from support to supplies.

    NAMI

    The National Alliance on Mental Illness is a national mental health organization that encourages and helps Americans with their mental health issues, and hopes to build better lives for the millions Americans affected by mental illness.

    BPA Health

    BPA Health is the largest network of mental health providers in Idaho and hope to connect all Idaho residents to personalized mental health and behavioral services.
